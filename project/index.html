About Me

Story through motion

I am a character animator with a BFA in Animation from BYU and professional industry studio experience.

I have done everything from storyboards to modeling to compositing, but my main focus is in animation itself. I am constantly trying to learn new programs and methods. I am enthusiastic about character driven narrative.

I do my best to be approachable, funny, friendly, and generous. I am a huge nerd and love TTRPGs. I like dice and legos.
